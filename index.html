Raechel Padua
Ontario, Canada

I've been doing 3D since 2012, starting through animation and games. Most of my work since has been building realtime environments and character pipelines across games, heritage, and interactive media, on projects that got surprisingly specific, like modelling concrete panels for an actual building facade or doing motion capture for Canada's first fully Deaf musical.

Outside of work, music has always been the other thing. I produce, mix, and play guitar. It keeps my creative thinking sharp and bleeds into how I approach timing and motion.
